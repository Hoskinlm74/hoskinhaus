Why We Care About This

At our tree business, we work with living systems that require patience, humility, and respect. We believe those same principles should apply to technology, privacy, and culture.

Modern platforms reward speed, dependence, and constant surveillance. We choose a different path — quieter, slower, and rooted.

Our Quiet Principles

  • Use tools that serve people, not profiles.
  • Prefer local ownership over centralized control.
  • Value durability over convenience.
  • Teach discernment instead of obedience.

What “DeGoogle” Means Here

It does not mean rejecting technology. It means refusing to let any single corporation shape our tools, our markets, or our thinking.

  • Privacy-respecting browsers and search engines.
  • Local-first systems whenever possible.
  • Minimal data collection.
  • Intentional digital habits.
